Triple Berry Crisp

A triple berry crisp is a delightful dessert that combines the tangy flavors of three different berries—typically strawberries, blueberries, and raspberries—into a sweet, juicy filling. This mixture is then topped with a crunchy, buttery oat streusel that bakes to a golden perfection. The contrast between the bubbling, juicy berries and the crisp, crumbly topping creates a harmonious blend of textures and flavors. Served warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream, a triple berry crisp is a simple yet irresistible treat perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cup blackberries
  • 1 1/2 cup rasberries
  • 1 1/2 cup blueberries
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • 1 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 1/2 cup butter, cubed

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. toss berries and sugar in large bowl
  3. Combine flour, oats, brown sugar, cinnamon and nutmeg. Cut in butter
  4. Press 1/2 of crumble into bottom of a pan
  5. Cover with berry mixture, then srinkle remaining crumble over berries
  6. Bake 30-40 minutes
  7. Enjoy :)
